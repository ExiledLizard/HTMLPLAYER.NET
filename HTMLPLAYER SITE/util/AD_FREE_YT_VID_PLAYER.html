AD-FREE Youtube Video Player

Hello, and welcome to the AD-FREE Youtube Video Player page of htmlplayer.net! What this page allows you to do, is to watch youtube videos without advertisements! To use this page, enter a link from any Youtube video in an input box below, then press the enter key on your keyboard (or press a button on the side of the input box) to load it in a small player window (has options to enter full-screen, e.c.t).
